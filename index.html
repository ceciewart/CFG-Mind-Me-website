Editor Picks

Untamed - Glennon Doyle
June 7th, 2022
Each month a member of the Mind Me team picks their favourite read. Our editors select some of the best mental health and wellbeing books so you can enjoy them too! June's book is Untamed by Glennon Doyle.

An intimate and powerful memoir, Untamed explores female empowerment, societal expectations and trusting our inner voice. Glennon's story is one of navigating divorce, forming a new family unit, and discovering joy and peace when you show up as your full, true self.

Doyle writes of her battles with anxiety and depression whilst facing misogyny, religious doctrine, an eating disorder and a marriage gone wrong.

We loved this soulful, tender memoir for its message of how each of us can learn to trust ourselves, make peace with our heartbreak and be braver than we ever thought possible.
